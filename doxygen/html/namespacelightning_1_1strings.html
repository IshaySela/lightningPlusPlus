Replace occurences of {} with the corresponding replacements pass as variadic template. If 1 replacement was provided and multiple <sepratorToken> where found, all occurrences of <sepratorToken> will be replaced with the single replacer.

Exapmles: format("Hello {}", "World") -> "Hello World" format("Hello {} {}", "World") -> "Hello World World" format("Hello {}","World", "break") -> ""
